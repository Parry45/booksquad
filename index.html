Overview

BookSquad is a mobile application that is focused on using technological solutions to help and enhance quick and easy access to purchase books.



Challenge Details

A new book shop just opened called BookSquad. They are looking to serve university students and corporate women between the ages of 25yrs - 55yrs. They do not want to own a physical store but want to develop a digital product that will help them market and distribute their books to the intended audience.
My task is to help them figure out what digital product will best serve their purpose and design a user experience & user interface for the said product.



Project Goals

1. To help BookSquad develop a digital poduct for their book sales business.

2. To help Univesity students and corporate woman purchase books quickly and with ease.

3. To help BookSquad reach a wider market.



User Goals

1. Choose Book they want to buy.

2. Place Order

3. Fund wallet, placing order via wallet instead of using a card/pay on delivery.



Competitive Analysis

I reviewed competitor’s overall app store and playstore ratings such as looking at the total number of downloads, user reviews and determining which features made them unique. Also, general advantages and disadvantages of website and mobile app digital products and the question of how would products are marketed and distributed to customers.



Survey Results

I performed a quick survey among my school mates and some women in the industry.The Survey was done with Google Forms. After collecting the results, I analyzed the data and came up with interesting conclusions.I had a total of 30 responses.
The results seemed to indicate that our users were interested in buying books online, but haven’t tried any Books store app yet. Then again I discovered revealed that the majority of users prefer to buy via mobile app to website.

The Survey Result resulted in my choice of using a mobile app for the book store.



User Persona

I created a user persona to visualize various aspects of users goals and motivations.



User Journey

I created the user flow after going through the user persons, surveys results and market research.



Sketch

I created a hand free sketch on how my user interface will be like after drawing out my user journey. Here I targeted to create an awesome user interface as well as a great user experience to make usage of the app comfortable.



Wireframes

After I made a few sketches, I started a low-fidelity design (wireframes) as wireframes are regarded as the skeletal view of a product. For this project, I used Figma for both the wireframe and high-fidelity designs. The wireframe helped me to focus on key functions, elements and actions before proceeding to the high-fidelity (hi-fi) designs.



High Fidelity Design

The wireframe made the hi-fi design quite easy and fun as I just needed to add a visual skin to the naked wireframes with color, icons and clear images.



Conclusion

The BookSquad project has been very fun as I have learnt new things regarding to user interface and experience design. I can boldly say this is a new experience, and still there‘s much more I‘d like to show in the future.
